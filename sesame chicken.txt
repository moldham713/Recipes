Ingredients:

1 bag gluten free chicken nuggets (mine is 1 lb.)
1 teaspoon vegetable oil
3 cloves minced fresh garlic
1/4 cup honey
1/3 cup soy sauce
1/2 cup ketchup
3 tablespoons brown sugar
2 tablespoons rice vinegar
1 tablespoon toasted sesame oil
2 teaspoons cornstarch
2 tablespoons sesame seeds
2 tablespoons sliced green onions
1 cup white rice

Instructions:
1. Prepare the chicken nuggets as instructed on the bag.
2. While the chicken is baking, prepare rice as instructed on label.
3. Combine the honey, soy sauce, ketchup, brown sugar, rice vinegar, sesame oil and cornstarch in a bowl.
4. Heat the teaspoon of oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the garlic and cook for 30 seconds. Add the honey sauce mixture and bring to a simmer. 
5. Cook for 3-4 minutes or until just thickened.
6. Add the crispy chicken to the pan and toss to coat with the sauce. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and green onions, then serve over rice.
