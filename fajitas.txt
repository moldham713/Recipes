Ingredients:
3 Boneless skinless chicken breasts
2 tbsp fresh cilantro, chopped
2 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp soy sauce
1/2 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp smoked paprika
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp ground cumin
1 tbsp lime juice

Steps:
1. Cut chicken breasts into halves.
2. Chop cilantro, squeeze limes, and combine them as well as the rest of the ingredients into a bowl.
3. In a large container mix chicken with the marinade. Use your hands to rub and make sure to cover all parts of the chicken.
4. Let marinade in fridge for 4+ hours.
5. Grill or cook on stovetop until done.
6. Serve however you want. Suggestions: burrito bowl w/ rice and beans, tacos with queso and salsa, nachos
