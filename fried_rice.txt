FRIED RICE

INGREDIENTS:
4.5 cups cooked and chilled white rice
Salt
Vegetable oil
2 eggs, beaten
3 Chicken Breasts, boneless & skinless
Olive oil
1/2 cup frozen peas
Half of a white onion
Handful of diced carrots
2 cloves garlic, crushed
A squirt of ginger paste
3 Tbsp oyster sauce
3 Tbsp soy sauce
1 Tbsp butter

Steps:
1. Lather some olive oil on a wide nonstick pan. Cut the chicken breasts into small bit sized cubes. Cook the meat on medium heat until you think it looks done then set aside.
2. Lather some vegetable oil on the same pan. Dice up the onions and carrots and throw them into the pan. Let them simmer for 5-6 minutes or until soft.
3. Throw in the garlic and ginger paste and sautee for around a minute. 
4. Add rice and peas, then pour the soy sauce and oyster sauce  and stir occasionally for 2-3 minutes.
5. Move the rice to the side and pour the eggs in the pan. Scramble them for 1 minute then fold into the eggs.
6. Add butter and stir to combine. Pour into a large container and eat for the rest of the week.
